Apple Applies for iWatch Trademark

01 Jul 2013

Following months of reports about Apple’s upcoming foray into wearable electronics, the company is now seeking to trademark “iWatch” in Japan, reports Bloomberg.

This is so exciting. I think Tim Cook made it clear that Apple was interested in this space. However, after iOS 7, I’m skeptical on how well this thing will look and work. I really hope Apple proves me wrong.

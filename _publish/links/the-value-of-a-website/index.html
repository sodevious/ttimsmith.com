The Value of a Website

26 Oct 2012

Your website is not a feature that you can half-ass. Spend some money. Protect your future. A good website works hard for your business. Much harder than many employees can offer.

What an amazing article by Brian Hoff telling businesses to value the work done by a website. In it, he talks about paying up to $100k for his own site. His examination of how online businesses are flourishing is great and hits you with a reality check. It's so true, our economy isn't in the pits, it's just that business isn't done the same anymore. You should definitely read this.

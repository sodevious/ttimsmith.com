This week, I had the pleasure of talking with Sam Kapila. Sam is a Designer and Educator, and from what I can tell, one of the first people to teach Responsive Web Design at a University level.

Now, I know that Responsive get's thrown around a lot as a buzz word, but Sam is working with Happy Cog and Paravel as her unofficial advisory board. It's great to see the support she's received in Austin for teaching her students the right stuff, and I had a blast talking with her.

Wunderlist is here to help you get things done. Whether you're planning an overseas adventure, sharing a shopping list with a loved one or simply keeping track of your daily to-dos.

Earlier last week, 6Wunderkinder released the new Wunderlist 2. I had never used any of their products before but, I'm so glad I finally did. Wunderlist 2 is an amazingly well-made app. The polish and simplicity is great and as you'd expect, you can reach your lists and to-do's from anywhere.

If you, like me, like to keep track of the things you need to get done and love gorgeous UI's, you need to get your hands on Wunderlist 2.
